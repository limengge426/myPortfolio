My Interests

I’m someone who finds joy in life’s many experiences, driven by a natural curiosity and a love for exploring the world. Whether it’s listening to music, diving into a captivating book, or getting lost in a good game, I always seek ways to engage my mind and soul. Swimming helps me recharge, while discovering new cuisines satisfies both my love for food and my adventurous spirit.

As an ENFP/INFP, I’m endlessly curious about the unknown, eager to try new things and embrace fresh challenges. I see every moment as an opportunity to learn, connect, and grow, approaching the world with an open heart and a mind full of possibilities.

Music

I have a deep love for all kinds of music, but classical music holds a special place in my heart. I started learning the piano and cello at the age of five, which shaped my appreciation for the beauty and complexity of classical compositions. Beethoven remains my favorite composer—his music is both powerful and deeply moving, inspiring me every time I listen. Among concertos, Rachmaninoff’s Piano Concerto No. 2 is my absolute favorite, with its sweeping melodies and emotional depth. For me, music is not just a hobby but a lifelong passion that connects me to something timeless and profound.

Gaming

I’m an avid gamer who finds storytelling and immersive worlds to be the heart of a great game. My favorite titles include Life is Strange, a deeply emotional journey that explores the beauty of choices and consequences; The Last of Us, with its gripping narrative and unforgettable characters; and The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, an epic adventure full of rich lore and breathtaking open-world exploration. I also enjoy games that challenge my creativity and strategy, like Stardew Valley, Hades, and Ori and the Blind Forest, each offering unique experiences. For me, games are not just entertainment—they’re a way to connect with stories, characters, and ideas that stay with me long after I put down the controller.
